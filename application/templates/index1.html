Project data analysis and reference prediction

Introduction

Our project deals with analyzing the previous years major project data of Computer Department of I2IT. It will suggest the experienced mentor in specific domain. This project will help students to choose mentor accordingly.

Motivation

For completing a project successfully we all need the best guidance so that we will get right direction to complete the project. From our projects, one will get various ideas about projects, its domain, and number of papers published in that particular domain. As it is always a difficult task for juniors to select appropriate domain for a project to get successfully completed, our project will assist them.

Objective

To extract the list of project titles and expertise mentor in specific domain To visualise most frequently chosen domain, faculty To get the abstract of previous years projects To analyse research paper submission statistics

Dataset Information

The dataset is taken from International Institute of Information Technology, Computer branch. It is a Categorical data with no duplicate entries. It contains 6 columns and 42 rows.
